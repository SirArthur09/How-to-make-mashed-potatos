How to make mashed potatoes

Ingredient Tools Recipe

Ingredients

Eight potatoes
Four tablespoons of butter
Water
Salt (optional)
Gravy (optional)

Tools

Stirring spoon
Pot (Big enough to hold the potatoes)
Strainer
Something to mash them with
Stove
Sink

Recipe

Take the potatoes and peel them.
Then take them and put them in the pot.
Cover them with water and put them on the stove.
Boil til tender.
Then take them and strain them in your sink.
Then put them back in the pot and then add butter and mash.
Add salt if wanted.
Serve with gravy.
